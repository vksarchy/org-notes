### üõ°Ô∏è Who Started the HRE?

* After **Charlemagne** built the **Carolingian Empire** in 800 AD and got crowned by the Pope, his empire split. His grandson's part, **East Francia**, eventually became the **HRE**.
* The actual **"start" of the HRE** in EU4 terms is **Otto I** being crowned by the Pope in **962 AD**.
* **Otto I = East Francia = proto-HRE.** Think of it as a formable nation that became the Empire via event.

> EU4 Analogy: East Francia is like a tag that gets a mission tree or decision to form the HRE via crown + Papal Relations > 100.

* **EU4‚Äôs HRE map mode** gives you a visual hint, but the real HRE was even messier.

### üèõÔ∏è How Did It Function?

In EU4 terms:

* The **Emperor** is elected by **7 Electors** (unless you stack IA and revoke the Privilegia).
* The Emperor protects the Empire and gets bonuses (extra manpower, diplomatic reputation, + force limit in HRE).
* HRE reforms can lead to:

  * **Centralised autocracy** (One-tag the HRE like forming **Renovatio Imperii**)
  * Or a **vassal swarm** (if you revoke the privilegia).
* **Religious turmoil** (like the Reformation) can cause the **League War**, which defines the Empire‚Äôs religion ‚Äî a major HRE event in EU4.

### üè¥ The Fall of the HRE

* **Date**: 1806 AD
* **Culprit**: **Napoleon Bonaparte**
* The French wrecking ball smashes through German lands.
* **Francis II of Austria** abdicates the imperial throne, ending the HRE to prevent Napoleon from hijacking it.
* **Post-HRE**: German states form the **Confederation of the Rhine**, and later, Austria and Prussia start fighting for dominance.

> EU4 Event Analogy: ‚ÄúFrench Aggression Cripples the Empire ‚Äì Emperor Abdicates‚Äù ‚Üí HRE tag is deleted, IA dropped to zero, member states independent.
