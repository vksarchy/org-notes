#+title: Doom Shortcuts

Action	Keybinding	Effect
Move subtree up	M-S-↑	Moves heading + body + children up
Move subtree down	M-S-↓	Moves heading + body + children down
Promote subtree	M-LEFT	Promotes heading and all subheadings
Demote subtree	M-RIGHT	Demotes heading and all subheadings
Mark subtree	C-c @	Marks entire subtree
Cut subtree	C-c C-x C-w	Cuts entire subtree
Paste subtree	C-c C-x C-y	Pastes subtree at point
Refile subtree	C-c C-w	Moves subtree to another heading
