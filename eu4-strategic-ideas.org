** Playstyle Differences

*** Aristocratic Playstyle
Focus:

* Territorial expansion
* Land dominance
* Military tradition
* Feudal stability.

Typical flow:

1. Early wars.
2. Expand borders.
3. Maintain manpower.
4. Snowball militarily.

Best regions:

* Eastern Europe
* Steppes
* Large continental powers.

---

*** Plutocratic Playstyle
Focus:

* Trade dominance
* Economic scaling
* Development tall play
* Stable internal politics.

Typical flow:

1. Secure trade node.
2. Expand trade influence.
3. Develop provinces.
4. Maintain professional army.

Best regions:

* Italy
* Low Countries
* Baltic trade zones.
