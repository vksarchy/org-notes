India's significant increase in defence exports is largely due to the growing demand for indigenous defence equipment, including:

1. **Missiles & Munitions** – BrahMos supersonic cruise missiles have been exported to countries like the Philippines, and more deals are in the pipeline. Other guided munitions and smart bombs are also gaining traction.

2. **Artillery Systems** – The **K-9 Vajra-T howitzers**, **Pinaka multi-barrel rocket launchers**, and **155mm artillery shells** are seeing international demand.

3. **Aircraft & Drones** – The **Tejas Light Combat Aircraft (LCA)** has attracted interest from multiple nations. Additionally, **combat and surveillance drones** (like the Archer-NG and TAPAS UAVs) are increasingly sought after.

4. **Naval Systems** – Offshore patrol vessels, fast interceptor boats, and corvettes are being exported to friendly nations.

5. **Personal & Infantry Gear** – India has been exporting bulletproof jackets, small arms, and protective gear.

6. **Electronic Warfare & Radars** – Advanced surveillance systems, electronic jamming solutions, and AESA radars are in high demand.
