
The co-working / flex office sector has been on an all-time high in India. wework was segment defining product and had been the polestar of sorts in this sector. There is a strong acceptance to this concept and will definitely continue its sustained momentum primarily because of the following:  
- Low Capex  
- Flexible / Short-term contracts  
- High Compliance Adherence  
- Workforce getting mobile and Hybrid becoming a strong preference  
- Community building
