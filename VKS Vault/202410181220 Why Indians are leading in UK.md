## Key Insights

The data presented in the image reveals a remarkable story of Indian success in the UK, spanning various sectors and socioeconomic indicators.

**Educational Achievement**
Indians in the UK are demonstrating exceptional academic performance, with 75% achieving A*-C grades at GCSE level compared to the national average of 65%[1]. This educational excellence is likely to translate into a highly skilled workforce in the coming years.

**Economic Success**
The median hourly pay for Indians in the UK stands at £13.45, surpassing the national average of £11.82[1]. This higher earning potential indicates strong economic integration and contribution to the UK economy.

**Property Ownership**
Indians are making significant strides in property ownership, with 74% owning their homes compared to the UK average of 63%[1]. This trend suggests a strong commitment to long-term settlement and investment in the UK.
