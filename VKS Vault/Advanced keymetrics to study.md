 
You’ve covered a **strong foundation**, but there are **additional advanced metrics, qualitative factors, and trading-specific indicators** that can enhance your analysis when using *The Economic Times (ET) India* for trading. Here’s an **expanded list** of what else to consider:  

---

### **8. Advanced Financial Ratios & Efficiency Metrics**  
- **Asset Turnover Ratio** – How efficiently a company uses assets to generate revenue.  
- **Working Capital Cycle** – Days inventory outstanding + Days sales outstanding – Days payables outstanding.  
- **Cash Conversion Cycle (CCC)** – Measures how fast a company turns inventory into cash.  
- **Net Debt to EBITDA** – Assesses how many years it would take to pay off debt using EBITDA.  
- **FCF Yield (Free Cash Flow / Market Cap)** – Compares free cash flow to company valuation.  
- **Capex/Sales Ratio** – Tracks how much a company reinvests in growth.  

---

### **9. Market Microstructure & Trading-Specific Metrics**  
- **Open Interest (OI) & PCR (Put-Call Ratio)** – For derivatives (F&O) sentiment.  
- **Cost of Carry (for Futures)** – Indicates arbitrage opportunities.  
- **Bid-Ask Spread** – Liquidity indicator (narrower = better).  
- **Short Interest & Delivery Volume** – Are traders accumulating or exiting?  
- **VWAP (Volume-Weighted Avg Price)** – Institutional buying/selling levels.  
- **Beta-Adjusted Liquidity** – How easily a stock can be traded without slippage.  

---

### **10. Qualitative & Event-Driven Factors**  
- **Management Guidance** – Future revenue/profit outlook in concalls (ET often covers this).  
- **M&A Activity** – Acquisitions, mergers, or spin-offs (check ET’s corporate news).  
- **Regulatory Approvals** – Especially for pharma, infra, and banking sectors.  
- **New Product/Service Launches** – Can be a growth catalyst (e.g., EVs, tech products).  
- **Supply Chain Disruptions** – Chip shortages, logistics issues (critical for autos, electronics).  
- **Geopolitical Risks** – How global conflicts/export bans affect the company.  

---

### **11. Technical Indicators (For Short-Term Trading)**  
- **Moving Averages (50DMA, 200DMA)** – Trend strength.  
- **RSI (Relative Strength Index)** – Overbought/Oversold signals.  
- **MACD (Moving Avg Convergence Divergence)** – Momentum shifts.  
- **Support & Resistance Levels** – Key price zones for entry/exit.  
- **Breakout Volume** – Confirms trend strength.  

---

### **12. Behavioral & Sentiment Indicators**  
- **Insider Trading Patterns** – Promoters buying/selling shares (ET tracks bulk/block deals).  
- **Analyst Upgrades/Downgrades** – Changes in target prices.  
- **Retail vs. Institutional Activity** – FII/DII buying trends.  
- **Social Media & News Sentiment** – ET’s market mood analysis.  

---

### **13. Macro-Fundamental Cross-Checks**  
- **Commodity Price Linkages** – Steel (for Tata Steel), Crude (for Reliance, OMCs).  
- **Currency Fluctuations (USD/INR, EUR/INR)** – Impacts IT, pharma exports.  
- **Bond Yields & Credit Spreads** – Affects cost of capital for highly leveraged firms.  
- **Monsoon & Agri Trends** – Critical for FMCG, auto (tractor sales), agrochemicals.  

---

### **14. Special Situations (Event-Based Trading)**  
- **Buyback Announcements** – Often signals undervaluation.  
- **Dividend Declaration Dates** – Can cause short-term price movements.  
- **Rights Issues & QIPs** – Equity dilution risk.  
- **Delisting & Takeover Bids** – Sudden price spikes.  

---

### **How to Track These on ET?**  
- **"Markets" Section** → Stock quotes, derivatives data, FII/DII activity.  
- **"Corporate" Section** → Earnings reports, management interviews, M&A news.  
- **"Economy" Section** → RBI policies, inflation, fiscal updates.  
- **"Technical Analysis" Columns** → Charts, trend analysis.  
- **"Wealth" Section** → Mutual fund holdings, SIP trends (indirect stock impact).
