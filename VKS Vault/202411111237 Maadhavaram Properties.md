### Madhavaram

Here are some notable apartment options in **Madhavaram**, Chennai, that cater to various preferences and budgets:

### 1. **Codename Talk of Madhavaram**
- **Type**: Gated community
- **Configuration**: 2 & 3 BHK flats
- **Area**: 5 acres
- **Price**: ₹6,500/sqft
- **Status**: Under construction
- **Features**: Surrounded by parks and kid's play areas.

### 2. **Vana Garden**
- **Type**: Residential complex
- **Configuration**: 1, 2 & 3 BHK flats
- **Price**: ₹5,998/sqft
- **Status**: Under construction
- **Developer**: Sivanta Foundations

### 3. **Lakshmi Homes**
- **Type**: Modern apartments
- **Configuration**: Various sizes available
- **Price**: ₹7,000/sqft
- **Status**: Ready to occupy
- **Developer**: Lakshmi Building Construction

### 4. **Charan Billbergia**
- **Type**: Low-rise apartment complex
- **Configuration**: Triple bedroom flats with balconies
- **Price**: ₹8,500/sqft
- **Status**: Under construction

### 5. **Hiliving Royal Evita**
- **Type**: Gated community
- **Configuration**: 2 & 3 BHK apartments
- **Price Range**: ₹64.13 L - ₹1.22 Cr
- **Status**: RERA approved and under construction

### 6. **Sidharth Crown**
- **Type**: High-rise apartments
- **Configuration**: 1, 2 & 3 BHK flats
- **Area**: 2.77 acres with a total of 384 units
- **Price**: ₹5,700/sqft
- **Status**: Under construction

### 7. **Casagrand Northern Star**
- **Type**: Luxury apartments
- **Configuration**: 2, 3 & 4 BHK units
- **Location Advantage**: Near the proposed Metro station and NH 45
- **Features**: Premium fittings and extensive amenities including a swimming pool and gym.
  
These options provide a mix of luxury and affordability, catering to different lifestyle needs in Madhavaram.
