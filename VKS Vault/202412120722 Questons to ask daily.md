It's awesome you're looking for ways to boost your mood and motivation! Asking yourself the right questions can be super powerful for gaining clarity and getting yourself psyched up. Here are a few questions to get you started:

**To Feel Good:**

- **What am I grateful for today?** Focusing on gratitude shifts your attention to the positive things in your life, big or small.
- **What made me smile or laugh recently?** Recalling happy memories can instantly lift your spirits.
- **What are my strengths and accomplishments?** Acknowledging your abilities and successes builds confidence and self-esteem.
- **Who are the people I love and who love me?** Remembering your support system reminds you that you're not alone.
- **What small act of kindness can I do for myself or someone else today?** Helping others (or yourself!) can create a sense of purpose and fulfilment.

**To Get Motivated:**

- **What are my goals and dreams?** Reminding yourself of what you're working towards reignites your passion and drive.
- **Why are these goals important to me?** Understanding your "why" provides a deeper sense of purpose and motivation.
- **What's the next small step I can take to move forward?** Breaking down large goals into smaller, manageable steps makes them less daunting.
- **What resources or support do I need to succeed?** Identifying your needs allows you to seek out help and guidance.
- **What are my past successes that I can draw inspiration from?** Reflecting on your accomplishments reminds you of your capabilities.
- **What obstacles might get in my way, and how can I overcome them?** Anticipating challenges helps you prepare and stay resilient.
