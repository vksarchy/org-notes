### Indian Army Infantry Regiments Overview

- **Madras Regiment**
  - Raised: 1758
  - Centre: Wellington, Tamil Nadu
  - Motto: "Swadharme nidhanam shreyah" (It is an glory to die doing oneâ€™s duty)
  - War Cry: "Veera Madrasi, Strike and Kill"

- **Rajputana Rifles**
  - Raised: 1775
  - Centre: Delhi Cantonment
  - Motto: "Veer Bhogya Vasundhara" (The brave shall inherit the earth)
  - War Cry: "Raja Ramchandra ki Jai" (Victory to King Ramchandra)

- **Rajput Regiment**
  - Raised: 1778
  - Centre: Fatehgarh, Uttar Pradesh
  - Motto: "Sarvatra Vijay" (Victory Everywhere)
  - War Cry: "Bol Bajrang Bali ki Jai" (Victory to Lord Hanuman)

- **Dogra Regiment**
  - Raised: 1877
  - Centre: Faizabad, Uttar Pradesh
  - Motto: "Kartavya anvatma" (Duty before death)
  - War Cry: "Jwala Mata ki Jai" (Victory to Goddess Jwala)

- **Sikh Regiment**
  - Raised: 1846
  - Centre: Ramgarh, Jharkhand
  - Motto: "Nischay kar apni jeet karo" (With determination I will be triumphant)
  - War Cry: "Jo bole so nihal, Sat Sri Akal, Waheguru is Victorious"

- **Jat Regiment**
  - Raised: 1795
  - Centre: Bareilly, Uttar Pradesh
  - Motto: "Sangathan vyatha" (Unity)
  - War Cry: "Jat Balwan, Jai Bhagwan" (The Jat is strong, Victory to God)

- **Parachute Regiment**
  - Raised: 1945
  - Centre: Bengaluru, Karnataka
  - Motto: "Shatrujit" (The Conqueror)
  - War Cry: "Balidan Param Dharma" (Sacrifice is supreme duty)

- **Punjab Regiment**
  - Raised: 1761
  - Centre: Ramgarh, Jharkhand
  - Motto: "Sthal Wa Jal" (By land and sea)
  - War Cry: "Jo bole so nihal, Sat Sri Akal"

- **Grenadiers**
  - Raised: 1778
  - Centre: Jabalpur, Madhya Pradesh
  - Motto: "Naam Namak Nishan" (Name, Salt, Mark)
  - War Cry: "Sarvada Shaktishali" (Always strong)

- **Sikh Light Infantry**
  - Raised: 1944
  - Centre: Fatehgarh, Uttar Pradesh
  - Motto: "Deg Teg Fateh" (Charity and Arms)
  - War Cry: "Jo bole so nihal, Sat Sri Akal"

- **Maratha Light Infantry**
  - Raised: 1768
  - Centre: Belgaum, Karnataka
  - Motto: "Duty, Honour, Courage"
  - War Cry: "Bolo Sri Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj ki Jai" (Victory to King Shivaji) and "Har Har Mahadev" (O Lord Mahadev, You are omnipresent)

- **Garhwal Rifles**
  - Raised: 1887
  - Centre: Lansdowne, Uttarakhand
  - Motto: "Ayudhya Kriti Nischay" (Fight with determination)
  - War Cry: "Badri Vishal Lal ki Jai" (Victory to Great Lord Badrinath)

- **Kumaon Regiment**
  - Raised: 1813
  - Centre: Ranikhet, Uttarakhand
  - Motto: "Parakram veerta" (Valour, Bravery)
  - War Cry: "Victory to Goddess Kali", "Bajrangi Bali ki Jai" (Victory to Lord Hanuman), "Dada Kishan ki Jai"

- **Assam Regiment**
  - Raised: 1941
  - Centre: Shillong, Meghalaya
  - Motto: "Assam Vikram" (Unique Valour)
  - War Cry: "Rhino Charge"

- **Bihar Regiment**
  - Raised: 1941
  - Centre: Danapur, Bihar
  - Motto: "Veerta Se Yudh Par" (Valour in battle)
  - War Cry: "Bajarang Bali ki Jai" (Victory to Lord Hanuman), "Har Har Mahadev"

- **Mahar Regiment**
  - Raised: 1815
  - Centre: Sagar, Madhya Pradesh
  - Motto: "Yash Siddhi" (Success and attainment)
  - War Cry: "Bolo Hindustan ki Jai" (Victory to India)

- **Jammu and Kashmir Rifles**
  - Raised: 1821
  - Centre: Jabalpur, Madhya Pradesh
  - Motto: "Ranvirta" (Valour in battle)
  - War Cry: "Durga Mata ki Jai" (Victory to Goddess Durga)

- **Jammu and Kashmir Light Infantry**
  - Raised: 1947
  - Centre: Awantipora, Jammu and Kashmir
  - Motto: "Balidan Hum Veer Lakshan" (Sacrifice is a characteristic of the brave)
  - War Cry: "Bharat Mata ki Jai" (Victory to Mother India)

- **Naga Regiment**
  - Raised: 1970
  - Centre: Ranikhet, Uttarakhand
  - Motto: "Parakram veerta" (Valour, Bravery)
  - War Cry: "Naga Victory to Goddess Durga"

- **Gorkha Rifles (Various Battalions: 1st, 3rd, 4th, 5th, 8th, 9th)**
  - Centres: Sabathu (Himachal Pradesh), Varanasi (Uttar Pradesh), Shillong (Meghalaya)
  - Motto: "Koi Band Maro, Butter tu aai de" (Live like one with power)
  - War Cry varies: "Jai Maa Kali", "Ayo Gorkhali"

- **Ladakh Scouts**
  - Raised: 1963
  - Centre: Leh Ladakh
  - War Cry: "Ki Ki So So, Lhar Gyal Lo" (Victory to God)

- **Arunachal Scouts**
  - Raised: 2010
  - Centre: Riyayang, Arunachal Pradesh

- **Second Scouts**
  - Raised: 2013
  - Centre: Lucknow, Uttar Pradesh

This covers all the regiments mentioned in the video, including their year of raising, regimental centers, mottoes, and traditional war cries.[1]
