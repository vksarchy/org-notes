- **c. 195 BCE:** Flees Carthage to avoid Roman extradition, first to Crete, then to the Seleucid court of Antiochus III in Syria[2](https://www.worldhistory.org/timeline/hannibal/)[3](https://dcc.dickinson.edu/nepos-hannibal/chronology)[5](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hannibal)[6](https://totallyhistory.com/hannibal/).
