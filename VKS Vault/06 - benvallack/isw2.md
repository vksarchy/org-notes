# Adjustments for deodorant if causes irritation

#untested #develop

If you get skin irritation from the bicarb in DIY deodorant, reduce
the proportion of bicarb and replace it with kaolin clay, arrowroot
powder, or cornstarch.

Replace half of coconut oil with shea butter for more soothing mixture.
