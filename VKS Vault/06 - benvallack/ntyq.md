# Widgy Pry Bar D9 Steels

The Widgy Pry Bar uses 'D-9' heat-treated stainless steel. As D-9
isn't widely recognized, I emailed them to ask about its properties.
They confirmed it is a proprietary steel that uses a triple-quench
heat treat process and is indeed harder than titanium.
