# Essential oil blends for soap

### Refreshing blend:
- Lavender – 5 drops
- Lemon – 4 drops
- Tea Tree – 2 drops
- Geranium or Palmarosa - 2 drops

### Energizing blend:
- Lemon – 5 drops
- Peppermint – 3 drops
- Tea Tree – 2 drops
- Rosemary – 2 drops

### Relaxing blend:
- Lavender – 6 drops
- Geranium – 3 drops
- Palmarosa – 2 drops
- Lemon - 1 drop
