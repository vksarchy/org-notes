# giving up a smartphone and deep thinking

#idea

When you abandon the habitual use of a smartphone an amazing thing
happens. You find you have much more time for deep thinking. Your mind
seems to able to process so much more and new ideas seem much easier
to come by. When combined with the replacement [[iydg|notetakingworkflow]] and hardware
([[ur43|small]] laptop, [[p3f2|pencil]]) the time once spent doom scrolling can instead be bundled up and used
wisely.
