# An alternative approach for EDC cameras

Instead of searching for the 'perfect camera,' it's possible that a
good option for many might be to think of an approach more aligned
with the 'counterparts' philosophy. Something like a Ricoh GR IIIx and
an Osmo Pocket 3 makes for a very compelling stills and video kit for
EDC, both being small enough to slip into trouser pockets in between
use.
