# Leroy study on attention residue

#source

P. 42 of Deep Work (paperback, Cal Newport) mentions a 2009 study by
Leroy that proves the devastating impact of switching tasks on our
mental performance. The study is clear: switching tasks or dealing
with interruptions reduces our performance.
