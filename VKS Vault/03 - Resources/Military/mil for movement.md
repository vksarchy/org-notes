### **Standard Military Terms for Movement:**

✅ **"Move out"** – General command to start moving  
✅ **"Advance to"** – Proceed to a location  
✅ **"Proceed to"** – Standard for movement in reports/orders  
✅ **"Push to"** – Often used in radio comms to direct movement  
✅ **"Shift to"** – Used for repositioning  
✅ **"Maneuver to"** – Tactical movement  
✅ **"Extract to"** – Moving to an exfiltration point  
✅ **"Rally at"** – Regroup at a specified location
