Some of the best psychedelic albums from this era include **The Beatles’ Sgt. Pepper’s Lonely Hearts Club Band**, **Pink Floyd’s The Piper at the Gates of Dawn**, **The Jimi Hendrix Experience’s Are You Experienced**, and **The Doors’ The Doors**.

[Psychedelic music has had numerous spiritual successors and revivals since the 1970s, including progressive rock, krautrock, heavy metal, psychedelic funk, neo-psychedelia, stoner rock, and psychedelic electronic music genres such as acid house, trance music, and new rave](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Psychedelic_music)[1](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Psychedelic_music). Some of the modern artists who have been influenced by psychedelic music include **Tame Impala**, **MGMT**, **Animal Collective**, **Flaming Lips**, and **King Gizzard and the Lizard Wizard**.
