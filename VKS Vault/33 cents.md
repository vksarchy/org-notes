### **Key Events and Transactions**

1. **1970-1995: Initial Property Ownership**
    
    - **09.11.1970**: A **will** was executed by Kappini Gounder in favor of his son **Natarajan**.
    - **04.06.1995**: A **family arrangement** was made between **Natarajan, Vijayakumar, Durai, and Ravichandran**.
    - **07.12.1995**: A decree was passed in **O.S.No.1679 of 1995**.
2. **1997-1999: Power of Attorney and Sale Agreements**
    
    - **07.07.1997**: **Vijayakumar executed a Power of Attorney** to **R. Chinnachamy**.
    - **14.12.1998**: **Chinnachamy (as Power Agent of Vijayakumar)** entered a **Sale Agreement** with **C. Natarajan** for **30 cents of land** at **Rs. 10,000 per cent**.
    - **06.08.1999**: Another **Sale Agreement** was made between **Varadharajan (new Power Agent of Vijayakumar) and S. Giri** for **Rs. 3,00,000/-**.
3. **1999: Financial Dispute Over Promissory Note**
    
    - **12.06.1999**: A **promissory note** was executed by **V. Padmavathy in favor of S. Giri**.
    - **18.08.1999**: A **cheque for Rs. 3,00,000/-** was issued but later **bounced** (intimation received on **04.10.1999**).
    - **06.10.1999**: **Legal notice issued** under **Section 138 of the Negotiable Instruments Act**.
    - **15.11.1999**: **Case filed against Padmavathy**.
4. **2005-2007: Property Disputes and Sale**
    
    - **06.10.2005**: An **order of attachment** was issued in **I.A.No.461 of 2005** in **O.S.No.436 of 2005** (related to property restrictions).
    - **15.07.2006**: Another **family arrangement** was made between **Vijayakumar, Padmavathy, and minor Karthik Siddharth**.
    - **16.02.2007**: **Sale deed executed by Varadharajan (as Power Agent of Vijayakumar) to Shanmugasundaram for Rs. 9,00,000 (33 cents of land).**
5. **2009: Legal Actions Taken by Vijayakumar**
    
    - **20.01.2009**: **Legal notice sent** on behalf of **Vijayakumar and minor Karthik Siddharth** to **Shanmugasundaram, Varadharajan, and Giri**.
    - **05.02.2009**: **Plaint (O.S.No.196 of 2009) filed against them** in District Court, Coimbatore.
    - **08.09.2009**: **Written statement filed by Varadharajan**.
6. **2015-2024: Legal Developments**
    
    - **02.03.2015**: **Varthamana Agreement** between **Vijayakumar and N. Prabhakar** regarding payment of money and lifting of attachment.
    - **2015**: **Transfer petition filed** to move O.S.No.1019 of 2009 to be tried with O.S.No.637 of 2010.
    - **13.06.2019**: **Restoration petition filed** by **Vijayakumar in O.S.No.196 of 2009**.
    - **2022**: Affidavit filed under **Section 5 of the Limitation Act** for condoning **244 days' delay** in filing the restoration application.
    - **02.02.2023**: **Final Order in I.A.No.1 of 2022 in O.S.No.637 of 2010**.
    - **28.05.2024**: **Encumbrance Certificate issued covering the period from 1993 to 2024**.

3. **Property Ownership Dispute**:
    
    - Conflicts over the **family arrangement agreements** and whether sales made by Power Agents were **valid or fraudulent**.
4. **Delay in Filing Cases**:
    
    - Petitioners (Vijayakumar & Karthik Siddharth) **sought condonation of delay** (244 days) under the **Limitation Act**.
