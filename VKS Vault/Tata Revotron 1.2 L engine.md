The Tata Revotron 1.2L is a significant petrol engine developed by Tata Motors for its passenger vehicles. Here are the key details about this engine:

## Engine Specifications

- **Type**: 1.2-liter, 3-cylinder, turbocharged petrol engine[6]
- **Displacement**: 1,199 cc (1.2L)[6]
- **Valvetrain**: DOHC (Dual Overhead Camshaft)[4]
- **Fuel Injection**: Multi-point fuel injection (MPFI)[6]
- **Emission Norm**: BS6 Phase 2 compliant[7]

## Performance

- **Power Output**: 118 bhp @ 5500 rpm[7]
- **Torque**: 170 Nm @ 1750-4000 rpm[7]
- **Transmission**: Available with 5-speed manual and AMT options[7]

## Features

- **Multi-drive Modes**: Offers Eco and City driving modes for optimized performance[4]
- **Technology**: Features a Smart ECU, eight-holed fuel injector, and stiffened crankcase[6]
- **Refinement**: Known for being extremely silent and vibration-free at idle[5]

## Applications

The Revotron 1.2L engine is used in several Tata vehicles, including:

- Tata Tiago
- Tata Tigor
- Tata Altroz
- Tata Nexon (in turbocharged form)

## Evolution

1. Initially introduced in 2014 as a 4-cylinder unit in the Bolt and Zest[6]
2. Later evolved into a 3-cylinder configuration for better efficiency[6]
3. Turbocharged version debuted in 2017 with the Nexon, producing 110 PS[6]
4. Further upgraded to 120 PS for the BS6-compliant Nexon facelift in 2020[6]

## Fuel Efficiency

- City: Approximately 17.44 kmpl[7]
- Highway: Can expect up to 18 kmpl[7]

The Tata Revotron 1.2L engine represents a significant step in Tata's indigenous engine development, offering a balance of performance and efficiency for the Indian market[1][5].
