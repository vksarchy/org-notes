How to maximize the intrinsic motivations? 
1. Identify out where you currently stand with each intrinsic motivators. 

* For curiosity 
1. To boost your curiosity - leverage from learning 
2. Immediate feedback (a flow trigger)

* For Mastery
1. To what degree you feel the desire to endlessly improve the skillsets your require for work? 
2. To what degree that will make you to succeed out
3. Identify all the skills & subskills that will advance your long term goals? 
4. Learn & practice those skills, practice the skills habitually... 

* Autotelicity (Audicity)
1. How often have you found out that when you weren't doing one of the main activities within your work, you were craving to do it? 
2. What are you good at? - Build a career around your strengths 

* Purpose
1. Is the org that i work within have a purpose? Am in align with it? 

* Autonomy
1. Over the last 30 days the degree to which the How, the Why, the When, the What related to your work felt like it was something in your control? Something you chose? 

So start with one intrinsic motivator that you're low & juice it up.
