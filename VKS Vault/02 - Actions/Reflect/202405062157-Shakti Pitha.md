## Shakti Pidam

Source: [Shakti pitha](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Shakti_pitha)

Shakti Pitha: 

Lore says that Shakti was carried over by Shiva throughout the universe and Vishnu used his Sahasra chakra and cut her body in 51 pieces.
