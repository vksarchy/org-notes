### **Major Projects in India**:

VA Tech Wabag has executed several major water infrastructure projects, including **municipal water treatment plants, desalination plants, and industrial effluent treatment facilities**.

#### **Key Projects in India**:

1. **Bhandup Water Treatment Plant (Mumbai)**
    
    - One of the largest drinking water treatment plants in Asia.
    - Treats **2,000 MLD (Million Liters per Day)** of water.
2. **Chennai Desalination Plant**
    
    - Supplies **100 MLD** of desalinated water to Chennai.
    - Second desalination project in Chennai after the **Nemmeli plant**.
3. **Namami Gange Project (Varanasi, Patna, Bhagalpur, etc.)**
    
    - Multiple sewage treatment plants (STPs) along the Ganges.
    - Supports the **Clean Ganga Mission** with several projects treating wastewater.
4. **Bangalore Wastewater Treatment Plants**
    
    - Built multiple sewage treatment plants (STPs) to improve water recycling in the city.
5. **AMRUT & Smart City Projects**
    
    - Working on **urban water supply and wastewater management** under government schemes.
6. **Chennai 400 MLD Tertiary Treatment Plant (TTRO)**
    
    - One of the largest wastewater reuse plants in India.
    - Supplies treated wastewater for industrial use.
7. **Petrochemical & Industrial Water Projects**
    
    - Provides water treatment for companies like **IOCL, HPCL, Reliance, and NTPC**.
