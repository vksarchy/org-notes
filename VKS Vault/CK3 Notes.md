### **Types of Vassalages**

1. **Feudal Vassals**
    
    - These vassals manage their own territories and pay taxes or provide levies based on their contract.
    - You can modify their obligations (taxes/levies) through hooks or diplomacy.
    - They are common in kingdoms/empires with a feudal government.
2. **Clan Vassals**
    
    - Found in regions with clan-based governments, particularly in the Middle East or North Africa.
    - They provide taxes and levies based on their opinion of you.
    - Maintaining high opinion is crucial to maximizing contributions.
3. **Tribal Vassals**
    
    - Tribal leaders pay taxes and provide levies based on their prestige rather than gold.
    - They are harder to manage if youâ€™re not also tribal.
4. **Special Vassalages**
    
    - **Grand Cities or Republics**: Often have unique obligations, with a focus on taxes rather than levies.
    - **Theocracies**: Controlled by clergy and often contribute based on religious alignment.

---

### **Managing Vassals**

1. **Keep Vassals Happy**
    
    - Use diplomacy, bribes, or gifts to improve their opinion.
    - Grant titles or honorary roles like Spymaster or Chancellor to important vassals.
    - Marry into their family for alliances and loyalty.
2. **Prevent Rebellions**
    
    - Watch for factions forming among unhappy vassals.
    - Use hooks to force compliance or adjust contracts.
    - Imprison or weaken potential threats through schemes or war.
3. **Modify Vassal Contracts**
    
    - Use a hook to negotiate more taxes/levies without angering the vassal.
    - Avoid overburdening powerful vassals; they may revolt.
4. **Manage Titles and Succession**
    
    - Avoid granting too many titles to a single vassal; they might grow too strong.
    - Use partition succession laws to split large vassal realms upon their death.

### **Gameplay Tips for Vassals**

- **Balance Power**: Keep vassals evenly matched so no single one can threaten your rule.
- **Imprison or Execute Traitors**: Justifiable actions wonâ€™t anger your other vassals.
- **Create Loyalists**: Establish a core group of highly loyal vassals who will support you in factions or wars.
- **Use Schemes**: Employ your Spymaster to uncover secrets or fabricate hooks on troublesome vassals.
- **Control Religion and Culture**: Converting vassals to your religion or culture can reduce friction.
