- **Origin of the “Alice in Wonderland Technique”**
    
    - Developed by L. Ron Hubbard (Scientology founder).
        
    - Described as intentionally using contradictory, confusing, or nonsensical statements to disorient a person.

    - Any message or command delivered in this state is accepted without critical thinking.

    - The brain instinctively clings to what appears stable—regardless of whether it is true.
