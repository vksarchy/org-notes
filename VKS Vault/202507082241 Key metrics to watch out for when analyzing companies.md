When using *The Economic Times (ET) India* for trading and analyzing companies, you should focus on key financial, operational, and market-related metrics. Below is a categorized list of essential metrics to track:

### **1. Financial Performance Metrics**  
- **Revenue (Sales Growth)** – YoY & QoQ growth  
- **Net Profit** – Absolute profit and margin trends  
- **EBITDA & EBITDA Margin** – Operational profitability  
- **Operating Profit (OPM %)** – Efficiency in core business  
- **EPS (Earnings Per Share)** – Profitability per share  
- **Dividend Yield & Payout Ratio** – Shareholder returns  
- **Free Cash Flow (FCF)** – Cash available after expenses  
- **Debt-to-Equity Ratio (D/E)** – Financial leverage  
- **Interest Coverage Ratio** – Ability to service debt  

### **2. Valuation Metrics**  
- **P/E Ratio (Price-to-Earnings)** – Valuation relative to earnings  
- **P/B Ratio (Price-to-Book)** – Stock price vs. book value  
- **EV/EBITDA** – Enterprise value relative to earnings  
- **PEG Ratio (P/E to Growth)** – Adjusted for growth rate  
- **Market Capitalization** – Company size & stock liquidity  

### **3. Liquidity & Efficiency Metrics**  
- **Current Ratio & Quick Ratio** – Short-term solvency  
- **Inventory Turnover Ratio** – Efficiency in inventory management  
- **Receivables Turnover Ratio** – Efficiency in collecting dues  
- **Return on Equity (ROE)** – Profitability from shareholders' equity  
- **Return on Capital Employed (ROCE)** – Efficiency in capital use  

### **4. Market & Sentiment Indicators**  
- **52-Week High/Low** – Stock’s price range  
- **Beta (Volatility Measure)** – Risk relative to the market  
- **Volume & Delivery %** – Trading activity & institutional interest  
- **FII/DII Holdings** – Foreign & Domestic Institutional Investor stake changes  
- **Promoter Pledge (%)** – Risk of promoter shares being leveraged  

### **5. Sector & Industry-Specific Metrics**  
- **Banking/Financials:** NIM (Net Interest Margin), GNPA/NNPA (NPA ratios), CASA Ratio  
- **IT Companies:** Revenue Growth (USD & INR), Attrition Rate, TCV (Total Contract Value)  
- **Commodity Stocks:** Realizations (e.g., steel/cement prices), Volume Growth  
- **Auto Companies:** Monthly Sales, EV Adoption Rate, Order Book  
- **Pharma:** R&D Spend, ANDA Filings, US/EU Market Growth  

### **6. Macroeconomic & Policy Factors (From ET News)**  
- **RBI Policy Rates (Repo, CRR, SLR)** – Impact on borrowing costs  
- **GDP Growth & IIP Data** – Economic health  
- **Inflation (CPI & WPI)** – Purchasing power impact  
- **Fiscal Deficit & Government Spending** – Sectoral stimulus  
- **Global Cues (Fed Rates, Crude Oil, Forex Reserves)** – External risks  

### **7. Corporate Governance & Risks**  
- **Promoter Holding Changes** – Insider confidence  
- **Auditor Remarks** – Red flags in financials  
- **Legal/Litigation Risks** – Pending cases or regulatory fines  
- **ESG Compliance** – Sustainability & ethical factors  

### **How to Use ET for Tracking These Metrics?**  
- **Stock Pages:** Check company-specific financials and ratios.  
- **Earnings Reports:** Analyze quarterly results commentary.  
- **Management Interviews:** Look for growth guidance.  
- **Sectoral Analysis:** Compare peers using ET’s comparison tools.  
- **Market Data Section:** Track FII/DII activity, bulk/block deals.
