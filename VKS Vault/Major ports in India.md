- Deendayal (Kandla) Port, Gujarat
    
- Mumbai Port Trust, Maharashtra
    
- Jawaharlal Nehru Port Trust (JNPT, also known as Nhava Sheva), Maharashtra
    
- Mormugao Port, Goa
    
- New Mangalore Port, Karnataka
    
- Cochin (Kochi) Port, Kerala
    
- Chennai Port, Tamil Nadu
    
- Kamarajar (Ennore) Port, Tamil Nadu
    
- V.O. Chidambaranar (Tuticorin) Port, Tamil Nadu
    
- Visakhapatnam Port, Andhra Pradesh
    
- Paradip Port, Odisha
    
- Syama Prasad Mookerjee Port (Kolkata), West Bengal
    
- Port Blair Port, Andaman & Nicobar Islands
