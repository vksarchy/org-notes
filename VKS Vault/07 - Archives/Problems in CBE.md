#coimbatore 
Problems and Potential Solutions for Coimbatore Citizens:
Infrastructure & Traffic:
 * Problem: Congested roads, traffic jams.
   Solution: Improve public transport, widen roads, implement intelligent traffic management systems.
 * Problem: Inadequate parking facilities.
   Solution: Build multi-level parking complexes, promote shared parking spaces.
 * Problem: Poorly maintained roads, potholes.
   Solution: Regular road maintenance, use of durable materials, strict quality control.
Water & Environment:
 * Problem: Water scarcity, especially during summers.
   Solution: Rainwater harvesting, efficient water management, promote water conservation.
 * Problem: Pollution (air, water, noise).
   Solution: Strict emission norms, promote eco-friendly practices, plant more trees.
 * Problem: Waste management issues.
   Solution: Improve waste collection and segregation, promote recycling and composting.
Civic Issues:
 * Problem: Inadequate public toilets, sanitation issues.
   Solution: Build more public toilets, regular cleaning and maintenance.
 * Problem: Encroachments on public spaces, footpaths.
   Solution: Strict enforcement against encroachments, reclaim public spaces.
 * Problem: Stray animal menace.
   Solution: Animal birth control programs, effective animal shelters.
Public Services:
 * Problem: Power cuts, unreliable electricity supply.
   Solution: Upgrade power infrastructure, promote renewable energy sources.
 * Problem: Slow government services, bureaucratic delays.
   Solution: Digitize government services, streamline processes, improve accountability.
 * Problem: Inadequate healthcare facilities.
   Solution: Upgrade existing hospitals, build new healthcare centers, improve healthcare access.
Other Issues:
 * Problem: Unemployment, lack of job opportunities.
   Solution: Promote skill development, attract investments, encourage entrepreneurship.
 * Problem: Safety concerns, especially for women.
   Solution: Improve street lighting, increase police patrolling, install CCTV cameras.
 * Problem: Lack of affordable housing.
   Solution: Promote affordable housing projects, regulate real estate prices.
