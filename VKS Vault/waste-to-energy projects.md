### Global Waste-to-Energy Projects

1. **Reju's Polyester Recycling Facility (Germany):**
    
    - **Overview:** Reju, a startup near Frankfurt, has developed a chemical recycling process that converts polyester waste back into high-quality polyester. This method allows for indefinite recycling without degradation.
    - **Innovative Approach:** Utilizes a sustainable catalyst requiring lower temperatures, enhancing energy efficiency. citeturn0news24
2. **Arc Ento Tech's Energy Plants (Australia):**
    
    - **Overview:** Founded in 2020, Arc Ento Tech is constructing three plants in Sydney to convert landfill waste into energy, providing an alternative to coking coal.
    - **Processes:** Employs biological methods using black soldier flies to process organic waste into products like insect meal and fertilizer, and mechanical processes to transform plastics into industrial fuel. citeturn0news33
3. **Sanfeng Covanta (China):**
    
    - **Overview:** A prominent WtE company specializing in municipal solid waste incineration and power generation.
