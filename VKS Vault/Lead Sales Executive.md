**About the Role**

WeWork India is seeking a **Senior Lead, Sales** to help lead its sales efforts in **Pune.** The successful candidate will be diligent with a proven track record in sales. They will be tasked with prospecting, outreaching, and generating midsize, and large company leads in their market. The self-driven and self-motivated candidate will be responsible for pitching, tour, and closing sales in all WeWork locations across their market.

**Roles and Responsibilities**

- 50% Lead generation and prospecting (i.e., cold calling/outreach, social media, networking, events, etc.)
- 25% Existing sales pipeline management
- 25% Inbound lead management
- Owning the occupancy of the allotted cluster(s) and working cross-functionally with various teams to ensure overall health is maintained
- Cultivating midsize and large business relationships
- Partner with the Inside Sales and Community teams to ensure successful placement and follow-up of new members
- Utilize innovative and creative sales tactics
- Touring and demonstrating WeWork locations and business solutions with potential members
- Intra City travel along with travel to different WeWork buildings regularly

**Education and Desired Qualifications**

- **7-8** years of **Commercial real estate sales experience (must)**
- **Should have good broker connects in Pune market**
- Ability to establish and nurture C-level relationships
- Ability to penetrate new markets
- Demonstrates good sales hygiene
- Experience with Salesforce is a plus
